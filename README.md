# Demon Lord’s Beloved Commander

A dark-fantasy **waifu VN + tactical card game** hybrid built in Godot 4.x with **Dialogic 2**.  
You play as a demon-possessed Hunter commanding a growing harem of unique waifus while navigating faction intrigue, purifying demon souls, and shaping the fate of the world.

**Core Pillars**
- Variable-driven character arcs and relationships
- Branching Dialogic 2 scenes
- Tactical card battles integrated into the narrative
- Graphviz DOT storyboards for narrative flow
- Persistent variable & storyline trackers
